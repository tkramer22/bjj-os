REMOVE THESE FIELDS FROM ONBOARDING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DELETE from onboarding flow:
- Goals (too vague, not actionable)
- Biggest Struggle (will come out in conversation)
- Strengths (most people don't know, especially beginners)
- Weaknesses (will reveal through questions they ask)
- Training Frequency (dynamic, changes week to week)
- Injuries (optional - can add in Profile later)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
KEEP THESE 5 FIELDS ONLY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FIELD 1: Name
- Label: "What's your name?"
- Type: Text input
- Required: Yes

FIELD 2: Belt Level
- Label: "What's your belt?"
- Type: Button selection (single choice)
- Options: White, Blue, Purple, Brown, Black
- Required: Yes

FIELD 3: Training Style
- Label: "What do you train?"
- Type: Button selection (single choice)
- Options: Gi, No-Gi, Both
- Required: Yes

FIELD 4: Height
- Label: "Height"
- Type: Picker/input
- Format: Feet/inches (with metric option)
- Required: Yes

FIELD 5: Weight
- Label: "Weight"
- Type: Picker/input
- Format: Pounds (with metric option)
- Required: Yes

4. Height (picker with haptic feedback)
5. Weight (picker with haptic feedback)
